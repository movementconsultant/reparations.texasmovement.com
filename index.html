STRUCTURE · DATA · FRAMEWORKS

Reparations, examined as structure.

Frameworks, data, and systems analysis on economic repair—long-horizon work, handled soberly, with clear boundaries.

Texas Movement Reparations Partners is the structural layer of the ecosystem. It provides education, frameworks, data, and systems analysis on reparations and economic repair. It does not provide legal advice or representation. It is for advocates, policy thinkers, institutions, and communities that need rigorous structural thinking about long-horizon questions. It works through analysis, modeling, and framework design, never rulings or guarantees. It connects to Consulting, applying the same systems discipline to a subject that demands care. The subject is serious. The work is framed accordingly—precise, historically literate, and clear about what it is and is not.

Areas of work

Structural analysis

Examination of the systems and conditions that shape economic outcomes, with attention to history, policy, and lived context. The aim is clarity, not verdicts.

Data & economic modeling

Models that make structural questions legible—flows, gaps, and long-horizon effects—so institutions and communities can see the mechanics behind headlines.

Framework design

Education & briefings
